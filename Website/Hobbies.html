Hobbies

Pool



I've been playing pool since I was 8 with my dad. I will never deny a game of pool if I

have the chance to, with nothing in my way. I don't play very often throughout the school year which makes every time I go amazing!






Soccer



I am no professional, but I am always happy to play, until I get tired. I mostly got interested in soccer in middle school

when I moved to a neighborhood where kids almost always gathered to play soccer for the fun of it, and I'd join them.






Card Games

I have only recently gotten into card games since I didn't know many until junior year. Junior I was introduced to several fun card games.

Including: euchre (my favorite), mau, b.s., and speed. Euchre was introduced to me at my brass band retreat junior year when there was a euchre

tournament and I learned to join the competition, although I lost in the first round. Speed was taught to me not long after, along with b.s..

I learned mau during the tour I went on with the brass band in Washington D.C. after a long day of touring and exploring.






Video Games



I have enjoyed video games since I was a little kid but it's mostly action video games that I go for. Now I usually play fighting or shooting games

such as: Call of Duty: Black-Ops 1/2 (where I typically play zombies mode), Halo Reach, Street Fighters Vs. Tekken, etc..
