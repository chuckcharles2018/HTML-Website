Vacations/Crazy Experiences

West Virginia Zoo






One day during summer vacation after my sophomore year, going into my junior year, my family decided to go to West Virginia for

the West Virginia Zoo. It wasn't nearly as big as the Columbus Zoo and Aqauriam, but there was a black swan there that wasn't enclosed

by a habitat, that I got a little too close to, that started chasing me until I got far enough away from it. Just as the traumatizing

moment was finishing up I turn to my loving family to see them all cracking up, but how could I be mad when it was my fault, so I joined in on the laughter.







Disney World

I went with the Central Crossing Marching Band to Disney World in March of 2017. It was also the first time I had ever gone to Disney World.

It was really fun and a completely different experience from anything I had done before. It was the first time I had ever been at an amusement

park where there was entertainment while you wait to get on the ride. My favorite ride definitely had to be Mission: Space in Epcot

because of how well the spaceship simulation felt. I also did my promposal to my girlfriend there in front of the castle at Magic Kingdom.
















Washington D.C

In the summer of 2017 I went on a tour with the All Star-Brass and Percussion in Washington D.C. where we played at the different memorials

there. The memorials we played at included: the Jefferson Memorial, the Lincoln Memorial, the Washington Memorial, and so on. The first day

of our tour we played at an armed forces ritirement home which was really awesome! We also got to sit in on one of the army band practices.

Even though we were on tour we still got a lot of time to explore Washington D.C. when we weren't playing at a memorial, which allowed me to go

places, like: the capitol building, the air and space museum, etc.
